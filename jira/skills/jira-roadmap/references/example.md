# ShopFlow — Product Roadmap

## Overview

ShopFlow is evolving into a complete commerce platform that helps merchants sell smarter, move faster, and scale with confidence. Our roadmap focuses on streamlining the buying experience, unlocking actionable business insights, and expanding into new markets and channels.

---

## Version 4.2.0 — Checkout & Analytics
**Status:** In Progress | **Target:** Q1 2026

This release makes checkout faster and more flexible while giving merchants better tools to understand their sales data. Multi-currency support and two-factor authentication round out the update.

**Highlights:**
- Express one-click checkout for returning customers
- Multi-currency pricing and payments in 25 currencies
- Redesigned revenue dashboard with daily, weekly, and monthly trends
- Mobile-optimized storefront with 2x faster page loads
- Two-factor authentication for all merchant accounts

[→ Release notes](release-notes-4.2.0.md)

---

## Version 4.3.0 — Marketplace & Growth
**Status:** Planned | **Target:** Q2 2026

Expanding ShopFlow into a marketplace platform where merchants can sell through third-party channels and leverage built-in marketing automation. This release also introduces an app ecosystem for custom integrations.

**Highlights:**
- Marketplace listing sync with major platforms
- Built-in email and SMS marketing campaigns
- App store for third-party integrations and plugins
- Customer loyalty and rewards program
- Advanced A/B testing for storefront pages

[→ Release notes](release-notes-4.3.0.md)

---

## Version 4.1.0 — Inventory & Fulfillment
**Status:** Released | **Date:** Q4 2025

Focused on giving merchants complete control over inventory and order fulfillment. Introduced real-time stock tracking, automated reorder alerts, and a unified view of orders across all sales channels.

**Highlights:**
- Real-time inventory tracking across warehouses and stores
- Automated low-stock alerts with configurable thresholds
- Unified order management for online and in-store sales
- Batch shipping label generation with carrier rate comparison
- Returns and exchanges self-service portal for customers

[→ Release notes](release-notes-4.1.0.md)
